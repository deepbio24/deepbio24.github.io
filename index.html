About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and scientist with a Ph.D. in Molecular Microbiology from Jamia Millia Islamia. Currently, I work as a Scientist in R&D at Micro CRISPR Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi. My focus lies in gene therapy, bacterial chromosome dynamics, and antimicrobial resistance. My research emphasizes DNA repair mechanisms, CRISPR diagnostics, and cell-based assays for translational microbiology.
