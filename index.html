About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and scientist with expertise in molecular microbiology, gene therapy, and antimicrobial resistance. My work spans across various domains, including bacterial chromosome stability, host-pathogen interactions, and molecular diagnostics. Currently, I serve as an Assistant Manager in the R&D department at Micro CRISPR Pvt. Ltd., where I focus on developing novel gene therapy solutions. My research interests include exploring DNA repair mechanisms, understanding microbial genomics, and leveraging CRISPR technology for biomedical applications.
